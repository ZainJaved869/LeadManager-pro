# 🚀 LeadManager Pro – AI-Powered CRM & Sales Management SaaS

LeadManager Pro is a modern, feature-rich CRM and Sales Management Platform built with Laravel 12, designed to help businesses, agencies, startups, and sales teams streamline their customer relationship management process.

With powerful lead tracking, pipeline management, AI-assisted content generation, customer management, analytics, and team collaboration tools, LeadManager Pro provides everything needed to manage the complete sales lifecycle from a single dashboard.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎯 Lead Management

* Create, manage, and organize leads efficiently
* Lead assignment and ownership management
* Lead notes, attachments, and activity tracking
* Import & export leads via Excel/CSV

### 📊 Sales Pipeline

* Interactive Kanban-style pipeline
* Drag-and-drop lead stages
* Custom sales stages
* Lead conversion tracking

### 🤖 AI-Powered Assistant

* AI Email Generator
* AI Follow-Up Writer
* AI Proposal Generator
* AI Lead Summaries
* Powered by Groq AI

### 👥 Customer & Contact Management

* Companies and contacts management
* Communication history tracking
* Meeting, call, and email records





### ✅ Task & Activity Management

* Team task assignments
* Priority and status tracking
* Reminders and notifications
* Calendar integration

### 📈 Analytics & Reports

* Lead conversion reports
* Revenue analytics
* Sales performance tracking
* Team productivity insights
* Interactive charts and dashboards

### 🏢 Multi-Tenant SaaS Architecture

* Company-based data isolation
* Subscription management
* Tenant administration
* Scalable SaaS-ready structure

### 🔐 Security & Performance

* Laravel 12 Framework
* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Secure Authentication
* Optimized Database Structure
* Responsive Design
